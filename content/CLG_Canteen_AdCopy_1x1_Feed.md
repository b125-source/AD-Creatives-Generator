---

> **HOW TO READ THESE:**  
> Each creative is broken into **Layout Zones** — exactly where each text element sits on the image.  
> Square format uses a centered or split composition (not the three-zone vertical of 9:16).  
> "TYPE SIZE" guidance: `XXL` = fills full width, `XL` = dominant, `L` = supporting, `S` = small/fine print  
> All caps text should be in **Bold Condensed Sans-Serif** (Impact style). Mixed case in **Clean Sans-Serif**.
